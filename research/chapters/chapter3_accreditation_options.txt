# Tennessee Accreditation Options for Child Care Centers

## Accreditation Information

Tennessee recognizes various national accreditation programs for child care centers. Accreditation is voluntary and goes beyond the minimum licensing requirements.

Common accreditation bodies include:
- National Association for the Education of Young Children (NAEYC)
- National Early Childhood Program Accreditation (NECPA)
- National Association for Family Child Care (NAFCC)
- Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI)

Accreditation may provide benefits in the Tennessee Star Quality Program.
